Understanding Crypto Card Rewards

Crypto Cashback

Earn cryptocurrency rewards on every purchase. Rates typically range from 1% to 8%, with higher rates often requiring staking or loyalty program participation.

Staking Benefits

Many cards offer enhanced rewards when you stake their native cryptocurrency. This can include higher cashback rates, rebates, and additional perks.

Travel Perks

Premium crypto cards often include travel benefits like airport lounge access, no foreign transaction fees, and travel insurance coverage.

Subscription Rebates

Some cards offer rebates on popular subscriptions like Netflix, Spotify, and Amazon Prime, typically paid in cryptocurrency.

Referral Rewards

Earn additional crypto rewards by referring friends and family to sign up for their own crypto card.

Special Promotions

Look out for limited-time promotions offering bonus rewards, increased cashback rates, or special perks for card usage.
